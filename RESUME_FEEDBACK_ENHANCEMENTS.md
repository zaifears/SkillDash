### 2. **Advanced ATS Scoring Algorithm**

#### Scoring Components (Total: 100 points)

| Component | Weight | Scoring Method |
|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| **Formatting** | 25 pts | Email validation, LinkedIn/GitHub URLs, contact info completeness, section headers |
| **Technical Skills** | 10 pts | Keyword matching against 50+ technical skills database |
| **Soft Skills** | 5 pts | Communication, leadership, problem-solving detection |
| **Certifications** | 10 pts | PMP, CISSP, AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, CCNA, Scrum Master, etc. |
| **Action Verbs** | 15 pts | 170+ strong action verbs analysis |
| **Quantification** | 10 pts | Numerical metrics, percentages, improvements detected |
| **Section Completeness** | 10 pts | Education, Experience, Skills, Projects validation |
| **JD Alignment** | 30 pts | Keyword matching with provided job description |

#### ATS Score Interpretation
- **85-100**: Excellent (passes all ATS systems, highly rankable)
- **70-84**: Good (likely to pass ATS screening)
- **55-69**: Fair (may pass ATS, but weak areas)
- **40-54**: Poor (likely filtered out by ATS)
- **Below 40**: Critical issues (major ATS failures)

## ðŸ“ž Support & Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Fixes

**Issue**: Low ATS score despite strong resume
- **Fix**: Ensure keywords are written naturally, not keyword-stuffed
- **Fix**: Check formatting - use standard fonts and sections
- **Fix**: Verify phone number and email format

**Issue**: Low JD alignment despite relevant experience
- **Fix**: Use exact terminology from JD (not synonyms)
- **Fix**: Ensure critical keywords appear in experience section
- **Fix**: Add missing certifications or skills mentioned in JD

**Issue**: Score seems unfair
- **Fix**: Score is based on market standards, not personal perception
- **Fix**: Compare resume to top candidates in your field
- **Fix**: Focus on specific improvement suggestions provided
